Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about BrewResume features and capabilities.

How does BrewResume's AI technology improve my résumé?
Our AI analyzes thousands of successful résumés and job descriptions to provide tailored suggestions for your content. It identifies impactful action verbs, recommends skills relevant to your target position, and helps you highlight achievements that align with employer expectations.
Can I import my existing résumé or LinkedIn profile?
Yes! BrewResume allows you to import content from existing PDFs, Word documents, or your LinkedIn profile. Our system will parse the information and populate the appropriate sections, saving you time and making it easy to enhance your existing content.
Are BrewResume templates ATS-friendly?
Absolutely. All our templates are designed with ATS compatibility in mind. We use clean layouts, standard section headings, and appropriate formatting that ensures your résumé will be correctly parsed by applicant tracking systems while still looking visually appealing to human recruiters.
Can I create multiple versions of my résumé?
Yes, our Professional and Career Pro plans allow you to create unlimited versions of your résumé. This enables you to tailor your document for different positions, industries, or companies without starting from scratch each time.
What export formats are available?
Depending on your plan, you can export your résumé as PDF, Microsoft Word (.docx), plain text for online applications, and JSON format (Career Pro plan only). All exports maintain proper formatting and are optimized for their intended use.
Is my data secure with BrewResume?
We take data security seriously. BrewResume uses industry-standard encryption and secure storage practices to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, and you maintain full ownership of all content you create.
