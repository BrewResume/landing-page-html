Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our pricing and features.

What happens after my free trial ends?
After your 7-day free trial period ends, your account will automatically convert to a Free plan unless you choose to upgrade to Pro or Team. Any resumes you've created during the trial will remain accessible, but premium features will be limited until you upgrade.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid plan until the end of your billing period. We don't offer prorated refunds for partial months, but we do have a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept PayPal and Apple Pay. For Team plans, we can also accommodate invoice payments and purchase orders.
Can I switch plans after I subscribe?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the change takes effect immediately and we'll prorate the difference. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Is my resume data secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices and never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. You can review our privacy policy for more details.
Do you offer discounts for students?
Yes! We offer a 50% discount on Pro plans for verified students and recent graduates (within 6 months of graduation). To apply for the student discount, register with your academic email address or contact our support team with proof of enrollment.
