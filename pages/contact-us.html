Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, our support team aims to respond within 4 hours.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our premium plans. If you're not completely satisfied, simply contact our support team for a full refund.

Do you offer personalized resume reviews?

Yes! Our Premium and Enterprise plans include personalized resume reviews by our career experts. You can also purchase a one-time review service separately.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. We don't lock you into long-term contracts, and you can easily resume your subscription later if needed.

Are my resume and personal details secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties, and you can request data deletion at any time.

Do you offer discounts for students?

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for students with a valid .edu email address. Contact our support team with your student ID for verification.
